Arrival Guide

How to find us

The building can be tricky to spot on your first visit. Follow these steps.

01

Head to Calle Príncipe 18

A short walk from Puerta del Sol and Plaza de Santa Ana, in the heart of Madrid's historic centre.

02

Look for the iron gate portal

The entrance has distinctive iron gate doors — you can see through them. Look for 100 Montaditos on your left and La Abacería del Príncipe on your right.

03

Use the intercom

Press the button for your specific hostel. If no answer, look for the porter in the small room behind the elevator on the ground floor.

04

Take the historic elevator

Our 120-year-old elevator is part of the charm! All 3 doors must be fully closed to work. Stairs are right next to it.
